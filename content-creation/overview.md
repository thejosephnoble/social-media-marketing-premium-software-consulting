# Content Creation Best Practices

## Tailoring Content for Consulting
Focus on educational, value-packed content: blogs, infographics, videos on topics like 'Optimizing Legacy Codebases'.

## Types of Content
- **Long-Form**: LinkedIn articles (800+ words) with data, examples.
- **Short-Form**: Tweets, carousels on pain points.
- **Visuals**: Diagrams of software architectures.

## Step-by-Step: Creating a Post
1. Research: Use Google Trends, industry reports.
2. Outline: Problem-Solution-CTA.
3. Write: Professional tone, keywords for SEO.
4. Visuals: Add charts.
5. Review: Ensure accuracy, no jargon overload.
6. Schedule: Use Buffer.
