# LinkedIn Guide for Premium Software Consulting

## Why LinkedIn?
80% of B2B leads come from LinkedIn. Ideal for networking with executives.

## Key Tactics
- Optimize profile: Use banner with services, headline like 'AI Consulting Expert | Helping Enterprises Scale'.
- Post frequency: 3-5x/week.
- Content types: Articles, polls, videos.

## Step-by-Step: Lead Gen Campaign
1. Identify targets: Use Sales Navigator to search 'CTO software'.
2. Create content: Write post on 'Top 5 Cloud Migration Challenges'.
3. Engage: Comment on prospects' posts.
4. CTA: Link to free audit download.
5. Follow up: Message connections.

Track with LinkedIn Analytics.
