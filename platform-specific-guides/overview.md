# Platform-Specific Guides

## Choosing Platforms for Consulting
Focus on B2B hubs: LinkedIn (primary), Twitter/X (trends), YouTube (demos), Facebook (groups). Avoid TikTok unless targeting startups.

## LinkedIn Tips
- Post articles on industry trends (e.g., 'AI Ethics in Enterprise Software').
- Engage in groups like 'Software Architects'.
- Use Sales Navigator for targeting.

## Twitter/X Strategies
- Share quick tips: '#DevOps Tip: Automate CI/CD pipelines'.
- Join chats like #TechTuesday.
- Thread complex topics like cybersecurity threats.

## YouTube and Video
- Tutorials: 'Step-by-Step Kubernetes Setup'.
- Client testimonials (anonymized).
