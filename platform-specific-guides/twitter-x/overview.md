# Twitter/X Guide for Premium Software Consulting

## Why Twitter/X?
Great for real-time industry discussions and thought leadership in tech.

## Key Tactics
- Hashtags: #SoftwareEngineering, #CloudComputing.
- Threads: Break down complex topics like 'Blockchain in Supply Chain - Thread'.
- Polls: 'What's your biggest DevOps challenge?'

## Step-by-Step: Trendjacking Example
1. Monitor trends: Use tool like Trends24 for tech news.
2. Create post: 'With #AI hype, here's how consulting can ground it in business value [link to blog]'.
3. Engage: Reply to influencers.
4. Amplify: Retweet shares.

Measure impressions and clicks.
